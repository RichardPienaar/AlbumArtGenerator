Album Art Generator

Creates randomised artwork based on scaffold images from Unsplash. At the time of writing, all photos from Unsplash are Royalty Free.
Since the image generation happens in your browser, you will need to screenshot the image to download it. If anyone has a solution to this problem (or would like contribute to functionality), feel free to pull the github repo.
